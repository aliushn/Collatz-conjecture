Collatz problem

By Nejc Galof

Graph showing the orbits of all numbers under the Collatz map with an orbit length of 19 or less.

The Collatz conjecture is as follows:

Number is even, divide it by 2.

Number is odd. Multiply it by 3 and add 1.

Repeat indefinitely.
Click the circle for showing sequence for that number, or input your own number.
